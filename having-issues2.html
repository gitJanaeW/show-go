Having Issues?

Your spotify ID may not be what you think! Double-check that your are inputting the right information here.

Before using your username, make sure you have public playlists available. If you've just made your first public playlist, it may take 5 minutes or longer for your playlist to be searchable. Spotify playlists are not considered public if they are:

  • Not a playlist you've made
  • A playlist you've made that is not set up to be public
